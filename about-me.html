About

Darragh Briscomb is a Web and Mobile Development Student at the University of Suffolk, and the LGBTQ+ Officer at the University of Suffolk's Student Union. He loves video games - Witcher 3, Skyrim, Spiderman, Banished to name a few - and Superheros (particularly Hawkeye) and thrives most when learning or experiencing new things. His main aim in life is to make the world slightly better, and is somewhat of a serial volunteer.

Darragh's main skills are:

  • Web Development (Mostly Front End)
  • Event Management
  • Advertising and Design

In his work as LGBTQ+ Officer, Darragh has hosted many events in coalition with the LGBTQ+ Society, and has been the team lead on a variety of SU events. In the upcoming future, Darragh will be the main contact for the University's version of LGBTQ+ History month, and is working closely with the Staff Network to accomplish this.

Outside of University, Darragh is an Administrator and Tech Lead on a gender-diverse Discord, with the aim of creating a safe space for trans+ individuals to express themselves without judgement. He is also an advocate for LGBTQ+ issues, and has spoken at conferences about Trans+ Mental health. He has also sat on the Tavistock and Portman AGM panel, sharing his story and taking questions from the audience.

Darragh dabbles in poetry in his spare time and often writes LGBTQ+ poetry during charged events such as transgender day of remembrance, or in response to the GRA consultation.

To conclude, Darragh strives for equality, but also for equity to ensure that those disadvantaged are lifted in order to achieve their true potential.
